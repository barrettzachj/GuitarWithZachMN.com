About Me

With 12+ years of teaching experience and over 10,000 guitar lessons taught, I teach everything from rock to classical to improv and songwriting. I've had students from age 5 to 75 - all musical and personal backgrounds are welcome!

To parents/guardians looking to enroll their kids:

Together we can help your kids discover their creativity and grow through music while building the foundational skill and knowledge that it takes to be a musician. My number one priority is making guitar lessons a positive experience. I strive to foster my student's love for music and build their confidence along the way. See my "10 Tips for Parents" to learn how you can help your child to become a successful student.
